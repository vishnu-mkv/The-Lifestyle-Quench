About Curiosity

Curiosity blog is a platform where users can also become writers, write posts and share their knowledge. Write about the curious things you find in your life and let others also know about it. Enjoy.

Server is hosted at Heroku.

Features

  • Completely RESTful.
  • Anyone can create an account.
  • Anyone can apply for writer.
  • Editable writer profile. Anyone can see your writer profile and feel free to edit your writer profile.
  • Crop your profile picture with a built in cropper.
  • A WYSIWYG (What You See Is What You Get) Editor integrated for writers to create posts.
  • Completely mobile friendly. Use the editor on your mobile and write on the go.
  • Turn on auto save and don't worry about losing your edits.
  • Submit your posts to get published.
  • Search for posts.
  • Posts are validated by the server for broken image links. Image links are also generated dynamically.

Packages Used

  • ngx-quill is an angular (>=2) module for the Quill Rich Text Editor containing all components you need.
  • ngx-image-cropper Image cropper for Angular.
